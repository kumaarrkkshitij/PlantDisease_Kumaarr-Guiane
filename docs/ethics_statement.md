# Ethics Statement: Plant Disease Classification (Vision)

## Potential Risks
1. **Misdiagnosis Risk:**  
   - The model may incorrectly classify a plant leaf disease, potentially leading to improper treatment.  
   - Users should treat the predictions as advisory and verify with expert agronomists when necessary.

2. **Uneven Performance:**  
   - Performance may vary across plant cultivars, lighting conditions, backgrounds, or image quality.  
   - Some rare or unseen plant diseases may be misclassified due to lack of training data.

3. **Overreliance on AI:**  
   - Users might over-rely on the model without human verification.  
   - This risk can be reduced by providing clear disclaimers and recommendations for expert consultation.

## Mitigation
- Include disclaimers in the user interface to clarify the advisory nature of predictions.  
- Encourage users to capture clear, high-quality leaf images with good lighting.  
- Continuously update and retrain the model with new images from diverse conditions to improve robustness.
